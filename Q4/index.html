What is Nash Equilibrium in this Game?

This is a game of strategy. You and the computer both choose to "Go" or "Stop" at the same time. The best outcome is to "Go" when your opponent "Stops".

A Nash Equilibrium is a state where neither player can improve their outcome by changing only their own strategy. Since there's no single "best" move, the optimal strategy for the computer is to be unpredictable. It randomly chooses between "Go" and "Stop" with a 50/50 chance. Your best response is to do the same!
